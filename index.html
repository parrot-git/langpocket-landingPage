How The App Works?

Get Started

Download LangPocket, set up your account, and select the language you're interested in learning. Customize your reminders to ensure regular practice.

Add Words

Everyday, add new words to your personal dictionary. Associate each word with its meanings, related sentences, images, and even your own notes for future reference.

Interactive Learning

Engage with our various practice exercises - from spelling and pronunciation practices to interactive sessions. Each exercise can be done individually or in groups, providing a versatile learning experience

Stay Organized

Track your progress with our efficient word grouping and scheduling features. Review sessions are planned for each word group, and reminders keep you on track. Continue expanding your vocabulary and mastering the language at your own pace.

Dive into our rich set of features, each meticulously crafted to enhance and support your language learning journey. Unlock your potential and experience unparalleled growth in your vocabulary and comprehension skills.
